About me

Hi, I am Hendrik and I'm a junior developer. I'm also a student at CTU training solutions, currently attending a course in Software Development. I've been tinkering on & with computers for the majority of my teenage and adolescence, for building my personal PC to game development.

  • Cleaning & Tidying

    I enjoy living in a clean and tidy living space, thus making it essential to actively maintain and clean my envirnment. So I suppose cleaning is one of my interests, usually just popping in a earphone and go at it. Over the year's the act of cleaning has become effortless and somewaht enjoyable.

  • Creative Projects

    I a person which enjoys creating something, it doesn't quite matter what medium it is. I particularly like DIY'ing and I've made a handfull of helmet's from my favorite media which I display, these where mostly made from cardboard and spackle.

  • Game Development

    I've always had a special and unrelenting interest in video games and making my own magnum opus one day, in the past I have tinkered with unity, source and Gamemaker. Nowadays I use Godot as it's open source and I enjoy coding with GDscript.

  • Cooking

    Welp I got to eat and eating good does wonders for a persons wellbeing. I've worked in the culinary industry in the past and accumulated some knowledge which helped me not to starve. I really enjoy a good Braai and making my own pizza at home.

  • Video Games

    In Highschool I played alot of video games and I was a avid gamer, nowadays it has taken a backseat in my day to day but I still play some games during the weekends.
